Avinesh Polisetty

Senior Research Scientist at Apple.

Senior Research Scientist at Apple working on Siri’s Natural Language Understanding. PhD in NLP and ML from TU Darmstadt, with research in summarization, recommendation, and personalization. MS and B.Tech from IIIT Hyderabad.

When I’m not at work, I write, travel, and work through my I’m Possible List. I’ve backpacked across Europe, done a solo bike trip across India, tried paragliding, skiing, scuba diving, and completed a Vipassana meditation course.

Previously worked at IBM Watson, Lexical Computing Ltd., and interned at RMIT Search Lab and Apple.
