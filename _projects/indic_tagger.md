An NLP toolkit for Indian languages supporting POS tagging, chunking, and named entity recognition for 9 Indian languages including Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, and more.

Supports CRF, BI-LSTM-CRF, and HMM models with F1 scores up to 98%.

**Technologies:** Python, NLP, Deep Learning, CRF
